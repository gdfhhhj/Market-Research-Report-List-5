<p><strong>The Electrical Discharge Machining System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-cut EDM</li><li>Forming EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) Systems are mainly classified into two types - Wire-cut EDM and Forming EDM. Wire-cut EDM uses a thin, electrically charged wire to precisely cut through the workpiece, making it suitable for intricate and complex shapes. On the other hand, Forming EDM uses customized electrodes to shape the workpiece through repeated discharges. Both types of EDM systems are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for high-precision machining applications.</p></p>
