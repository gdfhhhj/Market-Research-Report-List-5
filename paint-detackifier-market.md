<p><p>Paint detackifiers are essential products used in paint booths to capture overspray and prevent it from contaminating the surrounding environment. They come in two main types: powder and liquid. Powder detackifiers are dry chemicals that are typically sprinkled onto the surfaces where paint overspray will land. Liquid detackifiers, on the other hand, are solutions that are diluted and sprayed onto the surfaces. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different application methods and preferences within the market.</p></p>
