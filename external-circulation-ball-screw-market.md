<p><p>External circulation ball screw market includes three main types - End Cap Type, Intubation Type, and Spiral Groove Type. End Cap Type ball screws have end caps to protect the screws from debris, Intubation Type ball screws have tubes surrounding the screw to guide the balls, and Spiral Groove Type ball screws have spiral grooves on the screw shaft for smoother movement. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different requirements in various industries.</p></p>
