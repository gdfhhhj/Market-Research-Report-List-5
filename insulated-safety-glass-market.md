<p><p>Insulated safety glass market includes a variety of types, with the most common being double-deck and triple-deck. Double-deck insulated safety glass consists of two glass panes separated by a layer of gas or air, providing enhanced insulation and soundproofing. Triple-deck insulated safety glass takes it a step further with three glass panes and two insulating layers, offering even greater thermal efficiency and noise reduction. Other types in the market may include specialty designs or variations on the double and triple-deck structures.</p></p>
