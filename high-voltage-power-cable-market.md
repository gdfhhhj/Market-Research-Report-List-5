<p><p>High-voltage power cables are used to transmit electricity over long distances. The market is divided into three main types based on installation method: underground, overhead, and submarine. Underground cables are installed beneath the ground and are commonly used in urban areas where space is limited. Overhead cables are suspended above the ground on poles and are typically used in rural areas. Submarine cables are installed underwater and are often used to connect islands or to cross bodies of water. Each type of cable has specific advantages and limitations based on the environment in which they are installed.</p></p>
