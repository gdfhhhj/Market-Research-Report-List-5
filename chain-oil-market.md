Polyether type is a synthetic oil made from ethylene oxide and propylene oxide, providing high lubricity and stability. Ester oil type is also a synthetic oil, offering excellent lubrication properties and improved thermal stability. Each type caters to different industries and applications, providing a range of options for lubricating chains effectively.</p></p>
