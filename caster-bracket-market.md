<p><p>Caster bracket market types include ordinary steel plate stamping, cast steel forming, and die forged steel forming. Ordinary steel plate stamping involves cutting and shaping steel plates to create the bracket. Cast steel forming utilizes molten steel poured into a mold to create the bracket. Die forged steel forming involves shaping heated steel using a die to form the bracket. Each method offers different benefits in terms of strength, durability, and cost, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
