<p><p>Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) is a technology that uses insulating material, such as silicon dioxide, to separate a layer of silicon from the substrate. This technology provides several advantages, including improved performance, reduced power consumption, and enhanced radiation resistance. Additionally, advancements in SOI manufacturing processes, such as FD-SOI and RFSOI, are driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) Market is the development of advanced SOI wafers with ultra-thin silicon layers and improved electrical characteristics.
