Corrugated board, with its fluted layer between two flat liners, provides added protection for fragile items. Other materials used in composite cardboard packaging include recycled paper, chipboard, and honeycomb board. These different types cater to diverse packaging needs in industries such as food and beverage, electronics, and retail.</p></p>
