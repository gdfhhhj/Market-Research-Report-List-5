<p><p>Industrial Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber is categorized into Low Viscosity, Medium Viscosity, and High Viscosity based on their flow properties. Low Viscosity grades are thin and easily flowable, making them ideal for applications requiring intricate molds or detailed designs. Medium Viscosity grades offer a balance between flowability and stability, suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. High Viscosity grades are thick and gooey, providing excellent adhesion and durability, typically used in sealing and bonding applications that require strong mechanical properties.</p></p>
