<p><p>Carbon steel tubes are commonly classified into two types: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are manufactured by welding together pieces of steel to form the tube shape, while seamless tubes are made by extruding a solid billet of steel through a die to create the tube. Both types of tubes are used in a variety of industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing due to their strength, durability, and versatility. The choice between welded and seamless tubes depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
