<p><ul><li>Half Fenders</li><li>Full Fenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trailer fenders are available in two main types: half fenders and full fenders. Half fenders cover only half of the trailer wheel, providing basic protection from road debris and splash. They are often used in lighter duty applications. Full fenders, on the other hand, cover the entire wheel and provide complete protection from mud, rocks, and water. They are commonly found on heavy-duty trailers and offer enhanced safety and cleanliness. Both types of fenders cater to different market needs based on trailer usage and requirements.</p></p>
