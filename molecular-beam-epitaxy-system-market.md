<p><ul><li>Normal MBE</li><li>Laser MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Normal MBE and Laser MBE are two types of Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) systems used in the semiconductor industry. Normal MBE involves the deposition of materials onto a substrate under ultra-high vacuum conditions, allowing for precise control over the growth of thin films. On the other hand, Laser MBE utilizes a laser beam to heat the material for deposition, offering higher flexibility and control over the growth process. Both types of MBE systems play a crucial role in the development of advanced semiconductor materials for various electronic devices.</p></p>
