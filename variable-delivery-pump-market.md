<p><strong>The Variable Delivery Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Pump</li><li>Ball Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable delivery pumps are commonly found in two main types: piston pumps and ball pumps. Piston pumps use a reciprocating motion of a piston to pump fluids, allowing for precise control over the delivery rate. On the other hand, ball pumps utilize a rotating ball inside a chamber to control the flow rate of the fluid. Both types are widely used in various industries where the accurate control of fluid delivery is crucial, such as in hydraulic systems and chemical processing plants.</p></p>
