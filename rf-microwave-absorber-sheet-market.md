<p><p>RF microwave absorber sheets are classified into Low Permeability and High Permeability types based on their ability to absorb electromagnetic waves. Low Permeability sheets have a lower ability to transmit electromagnetic waves, making them suitable for applications where minimal interference is desired. On the other hand, High Permeability sheets have a higher ability to absorb and dissipate electromagnetic waves, making them ideal for applications requiring high levels of electromagnetic interference suppression. Both types cater to different needs in the RF microwave absorber market.</p></p>
