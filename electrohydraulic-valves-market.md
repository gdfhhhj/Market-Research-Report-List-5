<p><p>Electrohydraulic valves are categorized into different types based on their mechanisms and operations. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a nozzle to control hydraulic flow through a flapper mechanism. Jet action valves rely on jet forces to regulate hydraulic flow. Direct drive valves operate directly through an electrical signal without the need for additional components. Each type of valve offers unique advantages and is used in various applications based on specific requirements for precision control and efficient operation in hydraulic systems.</p></p>
