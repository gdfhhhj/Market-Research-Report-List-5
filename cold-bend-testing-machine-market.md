<p><strong>The Cold Bend Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Point Bend</li><li>4-Point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold bend testing machines are utilized in various industries to determine the bending properties of materials. The 3-point bend testing machine applies a force at two points while the sample rests on a third point, allowing for accurate measurement of flexibility and ductility. On the other hand, the 4-point bend testing machine applies forces at four points, providing more even stress distribution and allowing for the testing of larger and stiffer materials. Both types are crucial in ensuring the quality and reliability of materials used in construction, manufacturing, and research.</p></p>
