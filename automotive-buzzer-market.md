<p><strong>The Automotive Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezo Buzzers</li><li>Magnetic Buzzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive buzzer market consists of two main types: Piezo Buzzers and Magnetic Buzzers. Piezo buzzers utilize a piezoelectric ceramic material to produce sound when an electrical current is applied, offering high efficiency and durability. Magnetic buzzers, on the other hand, rely on magnetic field fluctuations to create sound, providing a loud and clear output. Both types serve as essential components in automotive applications, such as indicating warnings, alerts, and notifications to drivers and passengers.</p></p>
