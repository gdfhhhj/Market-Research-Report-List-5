<p><p>Vertical Launching System (VLS) market includes three main types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. </p><p>Hot Launch systems ignite the missile inside the launch tube, allowing for faster firing rates but requiring more complex venting mechanisms. </p><p>Cold launch systems use gas pressure to eject the missile before igniting its motor, reducing the risk of fires inside the launch tube. </p><p>Concentric canister launch systems feature concentric canisters that hold missiles vertically, enabling rapid and efficient firing capabilities.</p></p>
