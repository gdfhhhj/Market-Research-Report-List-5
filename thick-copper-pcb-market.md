<p><p>Thick Copper PCB market consists of three types: Single-sided Thick Copper PCB, Double-sided Thick Copper PCB, and Thick Copper Multilayer PCB. Single-sided Thick Copper PCB has a copper layer on one side, Double-sided Thick Copper PCB has copper layers on both sides, and Thick Copper Multilayer PCB has multiple layers of copper sandwiched between insulation materials. Each type caters to different applications and requirements, such as high power and heat dissipation capabilities. These PCBs are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and power electronics.</p></p>
