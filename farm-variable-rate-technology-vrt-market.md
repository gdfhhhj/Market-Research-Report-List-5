<p><p>Farm Variable Rate Technology (VRT) is a precision farming practice that enables farmers to vary the rate of inputs such as fertilizers, pesticides, and seed based on the specific needs of different areas within a field. This technology utilizes data from satellites, drones, sensors, and GPS to create prescription maps that guide machinery to apply inputs at varying rates. By optimizing inputs based on the specific conditions of each area, farmers can increase yields, reduce costs, and minimize environmental impact.</p><p>The Farm Variable Rate Technology (VRT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of precision agriculture practices, advancements in technology, and the need for sustainable farming practices are driving the growth of the market. Farmers are increasingly turning to VRT to maximize their productivity and efficiency while minimizing waste. In addition, government initiatives supporting precision agriculture and the growing awareness about the benefits of VRT are also contributing to the market growth. Rising demand for food due to increasing population and decreasing arable land is further fueling the adoption of Farm Variable Rate Technology.</p></p>
