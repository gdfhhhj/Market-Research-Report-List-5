<p><p>Metalcasting market consists of various types such as Gravity Casting, High-Pressure Die Casting, Low-Pressure Die Casting, and Sand Casting. Gravity Casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold. High-Pressure Die Casting uses high pressure to force molten metal into a die cavity. Low-Pressure Die Casting uses low pressure to fill the mold cavity. Sand Casting involves pouring molten metal into a sand mold. These market types cater to different industries and offer a range of advantages in terms of cost, efficiency, and quality.</p></p>
