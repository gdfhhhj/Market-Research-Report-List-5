<p><strong>The Truck Starter Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control Truck Starter Motor</li><li>Electromagnetic Control Truck Starter Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct control truck starter motors are designed to start the engine directly without the need for additional components. These types of starter motors are typically used in smaller trucks and vehicles. On the other hand, electromagnetic control truck starter motors utilize electromagnets to control the engagement and disengagement of the starter gear with the flywheel. These starter motors are commonly found in larger trucks and heavy-duty vehicles due to their ability to handle higher loads.</p></p>
