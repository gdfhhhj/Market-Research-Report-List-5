<p><p>Digital MEMS microphones come in various types based on their interface protocols. I2S interface microphones use Inter-IC Sound (I2S) protocol for digital audio transmission. TDM interface microphones employ Time-Division Multiplexing (TDM) for higher channel count applications. PDM interface microphones use Pulse-Density Modulation (PDM) for digital audio signaling. Each type caters to different needs and applications, offering unique advantages in terms of audio quality, power consumption, and compatibility with various devices, making them suitable for different use cases in the market.</p></p>
