Methylcellulose is a thickener and emulsifier. Carboxymethyl cellulose is used as a stabilizer and thickener. Ethylcellulose is a film-former and binder. Succinic acid is used as a flavoring agent and preservative. Each type of modified soya flour serves a specific purpose in enhancing texture, consistency, and overall quality of food products.</p></p>
