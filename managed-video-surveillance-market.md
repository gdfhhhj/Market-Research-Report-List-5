<p><p>Managed Video Surveillance market includes two main types: Analog Video Surveillance System and IP Video Surveillance System. Analog systems use traditional cameras connected to a digital video recorder for video storage and management. On the other hand, IP systems use internet protocol cameras that transmit data over an internet network for video storage and management. Both systems offer monitoring, recording, and remote access capabilities, but IP systems are more advanced and provide higher resolution and integration options.</p></p>
