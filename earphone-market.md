<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic earphones utilize a small electromagnetic mechanism to convert electrical signals into sound waves. They are durable, have good bass response, and are more affordable. On the other hand, Electrostatic earphones use a thin diaphragm suspended between two electrically charged plates to produce sound. They offer superior sound quality, greater detail, and clarity but tend to be more expensive. Both markets cater to different audiophiles based on their preferences for sound quality, durability, and budget.</p></p>
