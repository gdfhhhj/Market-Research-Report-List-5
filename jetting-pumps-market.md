<p><p>Jetting pumps are categorized into three main market types: deep well, shallow well, and convertible. Deep well jetting pumps are designed for pumping water from deeper sources, typically over 25 feet deep. Shallow well jetting pumps are ideal for extracting water from shallower sources, typically up to 25 feet deep. Convertible jetting pumps can be used for both deep and shallow well applications, providing flexibility for different water sources. Each type of jetting pump is specifically designed to efficiently pump water from different depths.</p></p>
