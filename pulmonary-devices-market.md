<p><p>Pulmonary devices market includes various types of devices such as pulmonary biopsy devices, endobronchial ultrasound (EBUS) needles, airway stents, airway extraction baskets, and single-use bronchoscopes. Pulmonary biopsy devices are used for obtaining tissue samples from the lungs for diagnosis. EBUS needles are used for visualizing and obtaining samples from the airway and surrounding structures. Airway stents are used to keep the airway open and facilitate breathing. Airway extraction baskets are used for removing foreign bodies from the airway. Single-use bronchoscopes are disposable instruments used for examining the lungs and airways.</p></p>
