<p><p>Semiconductor molding systems come in three types - Fully Automatic, Semi-automatic, and Manual. Fully Automatic systems require minimal human intervention as they are capable of operating and molding products independently. Semi-automatic systems involve some level of human involvement in the molding process, requiring operators to assist in certain stages. Manual systems rely entirely on human labor for the molding process, with operators responsible for every aspect of production. Each type of system offers varying levels of efficiency and control in semiconductor molding operations.</p></p>
