<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Bipolar Membranes</li><li>Heterogeneous Bipolar Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bipolar membranes are classified into two main types: Homogeneous and Heterogeneous. Homogeneous Bipolar Membranes are made of a single material throughout, providing uniform electrical properties. On the other hand, Heterogeneous Bipolar Membranes consist of different materials in separate layers, each with specific functions. Both types of membranes are used in various industries such as water treatment, electrodialysis, and electrolysis to separate ions and polar molecules. The choice between homogeneous and heterogeneous membranes depends on the specific application requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
