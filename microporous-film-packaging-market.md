<p><strong>The Microporous Film Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Perforation</li><li>Laser Perforation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microporous film packaging can come in two types: mechanical perforation and laser perforation. Mechanical perforation involves creating small holes in the film using a mechanical process, such as punching or pinching. Laser perforation, on the other hand, uses laser technology to create precise perforations in the film. Both methods allow for controlled airflow and gas exchange within the packaging, helping to extend the shelf life of perishable products like fruits, vegetables, and bakery items.</p></p>
