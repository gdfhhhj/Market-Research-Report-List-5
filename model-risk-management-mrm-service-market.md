<p><p>Model Risk Management (MRM) services cater to three main market types: Model Specification Risk Management, Model Implementation Risk Management, and Model Application Risk Management. Model Specification Risk Management involves ensuring that the model accurately captures the intended relationships. Model Implementation Risk Management focuses on the accurate translation of the model into operational systems. Model Application Risk Management involves monitoring the ongoing performance and effectiveness of the model in real-world scenarios. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in mitigating risks associated with financial models.</p></p>
