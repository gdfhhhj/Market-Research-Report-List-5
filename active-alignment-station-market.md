<p><strong>The Active Alignment Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>4 DOF</li><li>5 DOF</li><li>6 DOF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Alignment Station market consists of different types based on degrees of freedom (DOF). 4 DOF stations offer x, y, z translational movements along with rotation around a single axis. 5 DOF stations provide an additional degree of freedom for rotation around a second axis. 6 DOF stations offer the highest level of precision with x, y, z translational movements as well as rotation around three axes. Each type caters to specific alignment requirements in industries such as electronics, automotive, and telecommunications.</p></p>
