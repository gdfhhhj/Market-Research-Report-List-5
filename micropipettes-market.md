<p><p>Micropipettes are a commonly used tool in scientific research and medical laboratories for accurately measuring and transferring small volumes of liquid. The market for micropipettes is divided into two main types: Mechanical Micropipettes and Electronic Micropipettes. Mechanical micropipettes are manually operated and use a piston to draw and dispense liquid. Electronic micropipettes, on the other hand, are battery-operated and offer greater precision and convenience with features such as programmable settings and digital displays. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reproducible results in various scientific applications.</p></p>
