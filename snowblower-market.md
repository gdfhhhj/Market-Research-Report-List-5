<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Two-Stage</li><li>Three-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snowblower market is categorized into three main types: Single-Stage, Two-Stage, and Three-Stage. Single-Stage snowblowers are ideal for light snowfall and are easy to maneuver. Two-Stage snowblowers are more powerful and can handle deeper snow with ease, thanks to their auger and impeller system. Three-Stage snowblowers are the most heavy-duty option, designed to tackle the toughest snow conditions by using an additional accelerator to propel snow at a higher velocity. Each type caters to different needs based on snowfall intensity and amount.</p></p>
