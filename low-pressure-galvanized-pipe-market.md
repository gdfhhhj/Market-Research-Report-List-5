<p><p>Low pressure galvanized pipe market consists of two main types: hot dip galvanized pipe and cold galvanized pipe. Hot dip galvanized pipe is coated in molten zinc, providing a more durable and corrosion-resistant finish. Cold galvanized pipe, on the other hand, is coated with a layer of electrolytic zinc, making it less durable but more cost-effective. Both types of galvanized pipes are commonly used in various low pressure applications such as plumbing, irrigation, and construction.</p></p>
