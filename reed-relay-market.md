<p><ul><li>Single-In-Line (SIL)</li><li>Dual-In-Line (DIP)</li><li>SMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reed relays come in various market types such as Single-In-Line (SIL), Dual-In-Line (DIP), and Surface-Mount Device (SMD). SIL reed relays have a single row of pins extending from the relay, making them easy to install on a circuit board. DIP reed relays have two rows of pins for a more secure connection. SMD reed relays are designed for surface mounting directly onto the circuit board, saving space and allowing for automated assembly processes.</p></p>
