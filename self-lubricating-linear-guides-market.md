<p><p>Self lubricating linear guides come in various types, including Ball Guide Rail, Roller Guide Rail, and Others. Ball guide rails use recirculating ball bearings to reduce friction and increase precision. Roller guide rails utilize cylindrical rollers to achieve smooth and accurate motion. Other types may include air bearings or magnetic levitation systems. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of load capacity, speed, accuracy, and maintenance requirements, catering to a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
