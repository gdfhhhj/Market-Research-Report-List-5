<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flap valves are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I flap valves are typically used in low-pressure applications where a tight seal is not required. They are simple in design and cost-effective. On the other hand, Type II flap valves are suitable for high-pressure environments and provide a more secure seal to prevent backflow. They are constructed with more robust materials to withstand higher pressures and ensure reliable operation in challenging conditions.</p></p>
