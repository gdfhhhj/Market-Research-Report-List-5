<p><p>Content Management Systems (CMS) are software platforms that enable users to create, manage, and publish digital content. There are two primary market types for CMS: web-based and on-premises. Web-based CMS are hosted on remote servers and accessed through a web browser, offering scalability and accessibility. On-premises CMS are installed on local servers and managed within the organization's infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of businesses in managing their digital content effectively.</p></p>
