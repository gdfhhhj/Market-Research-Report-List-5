Artificial Insemination-Intrauterine Insemination (AI-IUI) involves placing sperm directly into the uterus to facilitate fertilization. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) is the process of using frozen embryos from a previous IVF cycle. Other market options may include egg donation, surrogacy, and preimplantation genetic testing.</p></p>
