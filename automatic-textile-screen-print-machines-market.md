<p><strong>The Automatic Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic textile screen print machines can be categorized into two main types: in-line and carousel. In-line machines are designed for high-volume production, where garments are fed through the machine in a linear fashion, allowing for continuous printing. On the other hand, carousel machines have multiple printing stations arranged in a circular pattern, allowing for simultaneous printing of multiple colors or designs on different garments. Both types of machines offer efficient and precise printing capabilities, catering to different production needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
