<p><p>The Online Transformer Monitoring System market includes various types such as Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA), Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring, Bushing Monitoring (BM), Tap Changer Condition Monitoring (TFC), and others. DGA involves monitoring the gases dissolved in transformer oil to detect faults. PD monitoring identifies partial discharges within the transformer. BM focuses on monitoring the condition of bushings. TFC monitors the condition of tap changers. These different types of monitoring systems help utilities and industries to prevent transformer failures and ensure efficient operation.</p></p>
