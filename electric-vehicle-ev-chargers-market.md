<p><p>In the Electric Vehicle (EV) Chargers market, there are two main types of chargers - On-Board Chargers and Off-Board Chargers. On-Board Chargers are built directly into the electric vehicle and are used to recharge the vehicle's battery when connected to a power source. Off-Board Chargers, on the other hand, are standalone devices typically found in public charging stations or at home to recharge the EV battery. Both types play a crucial role in providing convenient and efficient charging solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
