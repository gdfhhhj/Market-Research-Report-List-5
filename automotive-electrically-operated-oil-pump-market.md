<p><p>Electrically operated oil pumps in the automotive industry can be classified into two main types: integrated pump and separate pump. </p><p>Integrated pumps are designed to be directly attached to the engine and are often connected to the engine control unit for precise oil flow control. On the other hand, separate pumps are standalone devices that can be mounted anywhere in the vehicle, providing more flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types offer advantages in terms of efficiency, performance, and overall vehicle reliability.</p></p>
