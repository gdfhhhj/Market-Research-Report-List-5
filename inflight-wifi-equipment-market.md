<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inflight WIFI Equipment market includes various types of technologies such as ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG relies on ground-based towers to provide connectivity to the aircraft. Ka Band Satellite offers higher bandwidth and faster internet speeds by utilizing satellites in the Ka frequency range. Ku Band Satellite technology operates on a lower frequency than Ka Band, providing broader coverage and reliable connectivity for inflight WIFI services. Each type of technology has its own advantages and target market in the aviation industry.</p></p>
