<p><p>The Anti-Microbial Filter market includes three main types: Non-Woven Fiber Filter, Nano Fiber Filter, and Porous Polymeric Filter. Non-Woven Fiber Filters are made of randomly laid fibers that trap particles. Nano Fiber Filters use nanotechnology to create a high surface area for efficient filtration. Porous Polymeric Filters have a matrix structure that allows for the passage of air while trapping microbes. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of filtration efficiency, durability, and cost-effectiveness in various applications.</p></p>
