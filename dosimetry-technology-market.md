<p><p>Dosimetry technology market comprises three main types: Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescence Dosimeters (OSL), and Radiophotoluminescent Dosimeters (RPL). TLD uses crystals that emit light when exposed to ionizing radiation, OSL relies on light emission from trapped electrons, while RPL features a combination of radiation-sensitive phosphors and fiber optics. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, accuracy, and convenience, catering to different needs in industries such as healthcare, nuclear power, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
