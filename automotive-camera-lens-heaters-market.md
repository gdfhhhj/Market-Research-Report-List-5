<p><p>Automotive camera lens heaters are available in different types to prevent fogging and ice buildup on camera lenses. The most commonly used types are silicone heaters and polyimide heaters. Silicone heaters are flexible and provide uniform heat distribution, while polyimide heaters are thin and lightweight, making them suitable for compact spaces. Other types of lens heaters include options like thermistors or PTC heaters. Each type offers unique advantages for preventing visibility issues in automotive cameras due to weather conditions.</p></p>
