<p><p>Non Destructive Testing (NDT) in Aerospace & Defense involves various methods to inspect and evaluate materials and components without causing damage. Common types of NDT in this market include Ultrasonic Testing, which uses high-frequency sound waves to detect defects, Radiography Testing, which uses X-rays or gamma rays to penetrate and inspect materials, Magnetic Particle Testing, which uses magnetic fields to detect surface and near-surface flaws, and Eddy-current Testing, which uses electromagnetic currents to detect defects. Other methods such as visual inspection and dye penetrant testing are also used in this market.</p></p>
