<p><p>Hearing care devices market includes various types such as Behind-the-Ear (BTE) Aids, In-the-Ear Aids, Receiver-in-the-Ear (RITE) Aids, and Canal Hearing Aids. BTE aids are worn behind the ear and are suitable for various degrees of hearing loss. In-the-Ear aids are custom-fit to the individual's ear canal. RITE aids are smaller and sit behind the ear with a receiver in the ear canal. Canal aids are tiny and discreet, fitting completely in the ear canal. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of individuals with hearing loss.</p></p>
