<p><p>The household smoke alarm market consists of various types, with two popular categories being photoelectric smoke alarms and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms are designed to detect smoke particles using a light sensor, providing early warnings for smoldering fires. On the other hand, combination smoke alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies to detect different types of fires effectively. These two types cater to different needs and preferences, offering consumers a variety of options to choose from for their home safety.</p></p>
