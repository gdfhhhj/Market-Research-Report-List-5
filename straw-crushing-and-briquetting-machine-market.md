<p><p>Straw crushing and briquetting machines are available in three main types on the market: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines use a rotating mechanism to crush and compress straw into briquettes. Hydraulic machines apply pressure through a hydraulic system to compact straw into briquettes. Screw extrusion machines utilize a screw press to crush and mold straw into briquettes. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, output capacity, and ease of operation.</p></p>
