<p><p>Stainless steel flexible hoses are commonly available in two main types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are constructed by winding a strip of metal around a core, providing flexibility and durability. Corrugated hoses, on the other hand, feature a series of ridges or corrugations along the length of the hose, allowing for increased flexibility and better resistance to pressure. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, plumbing, and manufacturing for their versatility and reliability.</p></p>
