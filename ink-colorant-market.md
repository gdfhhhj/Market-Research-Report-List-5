<p><strong>The Ink Colorant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye</li><li>Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ink colorants can be classified into two main types: dye-based and pigment-based. Dye-based colorants are water-soluble and provide vibrant, transparent colors. They are commonly used in inkjet printing for their ability to produce intense colors. Pigment-based colorants, on the other hand, are insoluble particles suspended in a liquid base. They offer excellent lightfastness and are often preferred for applications requiring long-lasting, fade-resistant colors, such as packaging and signage. Both types of colorants play a crucial role in the ink market, catering to different printing needs and preferences.</p></p>
