<p><p>Tissue dispensers can be categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual dispensers are typically more cost-effective and simple to use, while automatic dispensers offer a more hygienic and convenient option. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with manual dispensers being more common in households and small businesses, while automatic dispensers are popular in public restrooms and commercial settings.</p></p>
