<p><p>Electrical insulation materials can be categorized into various types including electrical insulating resins & coatings, electrical laminates and molded products, film and composite materials, mica products, prepregs and impregnating insulation materials, and electrical tape. Electrical insulating resins & coatings are used for protecting electrical components, while laminates and molded products provide structural support. Film and composite materials offer high dielectric strength, mica products provide thermal stability, and prepregs and impregnating insulation materials offer excellent mechanical properties. Electrical tape is used for wrapping and insulating electrical wires and cables.</p></p>
