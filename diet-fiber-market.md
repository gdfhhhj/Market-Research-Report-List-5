<p><strong>The Diet Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soluble Diet Fiber</li><li>Insoluble Diet Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diet fiber market is categorized into two main types: soluble diet fiber and insoluble diet fiber. Soluble diet fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive system, aiding in promoting gut health and lowering cholesterol levels. On the other hand, insoluble diet fiber does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the stool, helping in the prevention of constipation and promoting regular bowel movements. Both types of fiber are essential for maintaining a healthy digestive system.</p></p>
