<p><p>Nitrocellulose membrane filters are categorized into three main types based on their pore sizes: below 0.45 µm, 0.45 to 6 µm, and above 6 µm. Filters with pore sizes below 0.45 µm are commonly used for fine filtration applications such as sterilization of liquids. Filters with pore sizes between 0.45 to 6 µm are typically used for general purpose filtration. Filters with pore sizes above 6 µm are suitable for particle retention and clarification processes in various industries.</p></p>
