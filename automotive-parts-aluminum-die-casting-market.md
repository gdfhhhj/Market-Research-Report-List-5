<p><p>Automotive parts aluminum die casting market consists of different types including pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, and semisolid die casting. Pressure die casting involves forcing molten metal into a mold cavity using high pressure. Vacuum die casting removes air from the mold cavity before casting to ensure a precise product. Squeeze die casting employs a vertical press to squeeze molten metal into a mold cavity. Semisolid die casting uses partially solidified metal to provide better mechanical properties and reduced shrinkage.</p></p>
