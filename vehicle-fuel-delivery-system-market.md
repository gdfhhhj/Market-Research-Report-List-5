<p><strong>The Vehicle Fuel Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Delivery Systems</li><li>Common Rail Delivery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle fuel delivery system market consists of two main types: Pump-Line-Nozzle Delivery Systems and Common Rail Delivery Systems. Pump-Line-Nozzle systems use a mechanical pump to deliver fuel through individual lines to each injector nozzle. Common Rail systems have a high-pressure fuel rail that supplies fuel to all injectors simultaneously. Pump-Line-Nozzle systems are simpler and cheaper, while Common Rail systems offer more precise control over fuel delivery and better performance. Both types are commonly used in vehicles, with Common Rail systems becoming more popular due to their efficiency and lower emissions.</p></p>
