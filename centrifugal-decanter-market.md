<p><p>Centrifugal decanters are classified into two main types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical centrifugal decanters are designed with a vertical drum axis and are commonly used for solid-liquid separation processes. On the other hand, horizontal centrifugal decanters have a horizontal drum axis and are typically utilized for liquid-liquid separation applications. Both types offer efficient and reliable separation of materials, with each being more suitable for specific types of processes based on their design and functionality.</p></p>
