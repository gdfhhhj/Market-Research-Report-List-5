<p><p>The Overhead Catenary System (OCS) market can be classified into three types based on voltage levels: Low Voltage OCS, Medium Voltage OCS, and High Voltage OCS. Low Voltage OCS typically operates at voltages below 1,000 volts and is commonly used in light rail and tram systems. Medium Voltage OCS operates at voltages between 1,000 to 25,000 volts and is used in urban transit systems like metro and subway. High Voltage OCS operates at voltages above 25,000 volts and is mostly found in high-speed rail systems for efficient power transmission.</p></p>
