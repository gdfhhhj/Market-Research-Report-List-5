<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Brake Caliper market is divided into two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers use a single piston to apply braking force to the rotor, while Multi-Piston Calipers utilize multiple pistons for increased braking performance and responsiveness. Single Piston Calipers are simpler in design and typically used for light-duty applications, while Multi-Piston Calipers are favored for high-performance vehicles that require more precise braking control and stopping power.</p></p>
