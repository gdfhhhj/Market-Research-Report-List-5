<p><p>Real-time Bidding (RTB) is a method used in digital advertising to buy and sell ad space on a per-impression basis. In an Open Auction market, all advertisers bid for ad space in real-time, with the highest bidder winning the spot. In an Invited Auction market, only selected advertisers are invited to bid on ad space, typically by publishers or ad exchanges. This allows for more targeted and exclusive auctions, potentially increasing the value of the ad inventory being sold.</p></p>
