<p><p>Gas coolers are available in two main market types - explosion-proof type and non-explosion type. The explosion-proof type gas coolers are specifically designed to prevent any potential explosions that may occur due to the presence of flammable gases. On the other hand, non-explosion type gas coolers do not have specific measures in place to prevent explosions. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the industry and the level of risk associated with the use of gases in the cooling process.</p></p>
