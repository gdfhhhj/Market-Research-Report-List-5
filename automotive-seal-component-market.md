<p><p>Automotive seal components play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of vehicle systems. Three common types of materials used in automotive seal components are EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer), NR (natural rubber), and SBR (styrene-butadiene rubber). EPDM is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability. NR is preferred for its elasticity and versatility. SBR is cost-effective and offers good abrasion resistance. Each material has unique properties that cater to specific requirements in the automotive industry, making them essential components in vehicle sealing systems.</p></p>
