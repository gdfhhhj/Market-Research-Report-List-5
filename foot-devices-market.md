Implants devices are designed to replace or support damaged or missing parts of the foot, such as joints or bones. Bracing devices are used to provide stability and prevent further injury by immobilizing or supporting the foot. Supporting devices include orthotic insoles, arch supports, and shoe inserts that help alleviate pain, correct posture, and improve overall foot function.</p></p>
