<p><p>Disposable electrosurgical pencils are available in two types: hand control and foot control. Foot control pencils, on the other hand, are operated using a foot pedal, offering hands-free operation and convenience for the surgeon. Both types of electrosurgical pencils are disposable, making them easy to use and hygienic for surgical procedures.</p></p>
