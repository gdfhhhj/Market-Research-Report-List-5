<p><strong>The Optical Grade Silicon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method</li><li>Float Zone (FZ) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Grade Silicon Market Types refer to the different methods used in the production of high-quality silicon materials for optical applications. The Czochralski (CZ) Method involves melting silicon in a crucible and slowly pulling a single crystal from the melt, producing low defect density material. On the other hand, the Float Zone (FZ) Method involves floating a zone of molten silicon on the surface of a solid ingot, resulting in high purity and minimal contamination. Both methods are utilized in the production of optical grade silicon materials for various applications.</p></p>
