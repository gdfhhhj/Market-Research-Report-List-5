<p><p>Edible packaging materials market types can be broadly categorized into proteins, gelatin, corn zein, wheat gluten, and others. Proteins such as collagen and soy protein are commonly used for edible packaging due to their strong barrier properties. Gelatin derived from collagen is another popular choice for its flexibility and biodegradability. Corn zein is a biodegradable and edible protein derived from corn, while wheat gluten is a protein extracted from wheat that is also used as a packaging material. Other materials used in edible packaging include alginate, chitosan, and pectin.</p></p>
