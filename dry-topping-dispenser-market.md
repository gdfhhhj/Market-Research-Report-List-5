<p><p>The Dry Topping Dispenser market can be divided into two main types: Wall-Mount and Countertop dispensers. Wall-Mount dispensers are commonly installed directly onto walls, providing a space-saving solution for smaller businesses or tight spaces. They are often used in self-serve stations for customers to easily access toppings. Countertop dispensers, on the other hand, are placed directly on countertops and are more suitable for larger establishments with higher customer traffic. Both types offer convenient and efficient ways to dispense dry toppings in various food service settings.</p></p>
