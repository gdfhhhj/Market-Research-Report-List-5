<p><p>Noncontact temperature measurement market is categorized into two main types: Infrared temperature measurement and Optical Fiber temperature measurement. Infrared temperature measurement uses infrared light to measure the temperature of an object without making physical contact. This method is commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, Optical Fiber temperature measurement uses optical fibers to measure temperature, which is particularly useful in monitoring temperature in hard-to-reach or hazardous environments. Both types offer accurate and efficient temperature measurement solutions for various industries.</p></p>
