<p><strong>The Specialty Fuel Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deposit Control Additives</li><li>Cetane Improvers</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Lubricity Improvers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deposit control additives are used to prevent the build-up of deposits in the fuel system. Cetane improvers increase the cetane number of diesel fuel, improving combustion efficiency. Antioxidants help to prevent fuel oxidation and degradation. Lubricity improvers enhance the lubricating properties of fuel, reducing wear on fuel system components. Other specialty fuel additives may include cold flow improvers, corrosion inhibitors, and biocides. Each type of additive serves a specific purpose in improving fuel quality and performance.</p></p>
