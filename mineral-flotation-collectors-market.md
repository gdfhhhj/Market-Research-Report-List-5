<p><p>Mineral flotation collectors are chemicals used in the flotation process to attract and adhere to mineral particles, allowing them to be separated and recovered. There are three main types of flotation collectors: nonionic collectors, anionic collectors, and cationic collectors. Nonionic collectors have no charge and are often used for non-metallic minerals. Anionic collectors have a negative charge and are typically used for oxide minerals. Cationic collectors have a positive charge and are commonly used for sulfide minerals. Each type of collector is tailored to specific mineral types for optimal flotation efficiency.</p></p>
