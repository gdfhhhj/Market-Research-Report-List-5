<p><ul><li>Peripheral Cutting Edge</li><li>End Cutting Edge</li><li>Shank and Neck Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid milling cutters are categorized into different types based on their cutting edge and parts. Peripheral cutting edge cutters have cutting edges along the circumference for cutting the workpiece. End cutting edge cutters have cutting edges at the tip for cutting into the workpiece. Shank and neck parts cutters have a shank for attaching to the machine and a neck that connects the shank to the cutting edge. These different market types cater to various cutting needs and applications in the machining industry.</p></p>
