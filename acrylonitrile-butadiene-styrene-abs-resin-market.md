<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) Resin is produced through two main types of processes, Emulsion Graft Copolymerization and Bulk Copolymerization. Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves forming a graft copolymer by reacting monomers in an emulsion, resulting in improved impact resistance and processability. On the other hand, Bulk Copolymerization involves polymerizing the monomers in bulk, leading to a higher molecular weight and better thermal and mechanical properties. Both processes play a crucial role in the ABS resin market by providing different characteristics for diverse applications.</p></p>
