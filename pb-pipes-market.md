<p><strong>The PB Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PB Pipes Market Types include Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H pipes are made from pure Polybutene-1 polymer while PB-R pipes are a blend of Polybutene-1 and other co-monomers. PB-H pipes offer high heat resistance and long-term durability, making them ideal for hot water applications. On the other hand, PB-R pipes offer improved flexibility and impact resistance, suitable for cold water installations. Both types of PB pipes are known for their corrosion resistance and non-toxic properties, making them popular choices in the construction and plumbing industries.</p></p>
