S316L Grade is a low carbon version of S316, providing better weldability and corrosion resistance. S304 Grade is commonly used for general applications due to its good mechanical properties and cost-effectiveness. These market types cater to various needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
