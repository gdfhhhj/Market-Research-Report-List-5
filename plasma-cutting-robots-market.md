<p><p>Plasma cutting robots are classified based on the number of axes they operate on. 4-axis robots can move in X, Y, Z, and rotate around a vertical axis. 5-axis robots add a rotating wrist for more maneuverability. 6-axis robots add a wrist that can rotate around the tool axis. 7-axis robots have an additional wrist for even more complex movements. In addition to these types, there are other robots in the market that have unique axis configurations to suit specific cutting requirements.</p></p>
