<p><p>Optical branching devices are essential components in fiber optic communication networks. One type of optical branching device is Fused Biconical Taper (FBC), which provides efficient splitting of optical signals. Another type is Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC), which offers lower insertion loss and higher port count compared to FBC devices. Both FBC and PLC branching devices are widely used in various applications such as telecommunication, broadband, and data center networks. They play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and high-performance optical communication systems.</p></p>
