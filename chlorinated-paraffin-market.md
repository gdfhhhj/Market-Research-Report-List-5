<p><p>Chlorinated Paraffin (CP) is categorized into different types based on its chlorine content, with CP-42, CP-52, and CP-70 being the most common. CP-42 contains around 42% chlorine, CP-52 contains around 52% chlorine, and CP-70 contains around 70% chlorine. Each type has different applications and properties, with CP-42 being used for industrial lubricants, CP-52 for metal working fluids, and CP-70 for flame retardants. Other types of Chlorinated Paraffin may have chlorine content outside of these ranges and are used for various other purposes.</p></p>
