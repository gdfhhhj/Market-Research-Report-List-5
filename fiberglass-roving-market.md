<p><strong>The Fiberglass Roving Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li><li>Chopped Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass roving is divided into three main types based on length and structure: Single-end roving, Multi-end roving, and Chopped roving. Single-end roving consists of continuous fibers wound together into a single strand, suitable for hand lay-up processes. Multi-end roving is made up of multiple strands of continuous fibers wound together, commonly used in filament winding and pultrusion processes. Chopped roving consists of short lengths of fibers bonded together, ideal for spray-up and molding applications. Each type of roving caters to specific manufacturing needs in various industries.</p></p>
