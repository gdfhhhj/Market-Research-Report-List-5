<p><p>The commercial solar storage market has various applications including grid-tie, backup, self-consumption, and off-grid systems. Self-consumption systems enable businesses to store excess energy generated by their solar panels for later use. Off-grid systems operate independently of the utility grid, making them ideal for remote locations or areas with unreliable power sources.</p></p>
