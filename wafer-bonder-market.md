<p><p>Wafer bonders are equipment used in the semiconductor industry to bond two semiconductor wafers together. There are two main types of wafer bonders: Semi-Automated Wafer Bonder and Automated Wafer Bonder. </p><p>Semi-Automated Wafer Bonder requires manual intervention for loading and unloading wafers, as well as process control. On the other hand, Automated Wafer Bonder is equipped with robotic arms and advanced automation features that enable it to operate without constant human supervision. Automated bonders are typically faster and more efficient than semi-automated bonders, making them suitable for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
