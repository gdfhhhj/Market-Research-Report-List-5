<p><p>The Aircraft Propeller Market consists of various types of propellers including Fixed-Pitch Propeller, Test Club Propeller, Ground-Adjustable Propeller, Controllable-Pitch Propeller, Constant-Speed Propeller, Feathering Propeller, and Reverse-Pitch Propeller. Fixed-Pitch Propellers have blades that cannot be adjusted, whereas Test Club Propellers are used for testing purposes. Ground-Adjustable Propellers can be adjusted on the ground, while Controllable-Pitch Propellers can be adjusted in flight. Constant-Speed Propellers maintain a constant speed, Feathering Propellers reduce drag in case of engine failure, and Reverse-Pitch Propellers provide reverse thrust for braking.</p></p>
