<p><p>Automotive Multi-wheel Drive Systems come in two main types: Manual and Automatic. Manual Multi-Wheel Drive Systems require the driver to manually engage the system to distribute power to all four wheels, providing more control in varying road conditions. On the other hand, Automatic Multi-Wheel Drive Systems automatically distribute power to the wheels that need it most, without the driver needing to intervene. Both systems offer increased traction and stability, improving overall performance and safety on different terrains.</p></p>
