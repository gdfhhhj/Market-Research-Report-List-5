<p><p>Slag-based, rock-based, fly ash-based, and ferro-sialate-based are different types of geopolymer binders used in various construction applications. Slag-based binders are made from industrial by-products, rock-based binders are derived from naturally occurring minerals, fly ash-based binders utilize waste material from coal combustion, and ferro-sialate-based binders are formed from a combination of iron-rich materials. Each type of geopolymer binder offers unique properties and benefits, making them suitable for different projects and environments.</p></p>
