<p><p>Polyurethane casting resin market types include clear resin and opaque resin. Clear resin is a transparent resin that allows light to pass through, making it ideal for creating objects that require a see-through finish. On the other hand, opaque resin is a solid-colored resin that does not allow light to pass through, providing a more solid and dense appearance to the final product. Both clear and opaque resins offer unique properties and are used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
