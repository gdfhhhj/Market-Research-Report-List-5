<p><strong>The Alkaline Fuel Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cell</li><li>Fixed Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cell</li><li>Dissolved Fuel Alkaline Fuel Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alkaline Fuel Cells are classified into three main types based on the electrolyte used in the fuel cell: Circulating Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cell, Fixed Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cell, and Dissolved Fuel Alkaline Fuel Cell. In Circulating Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cells, the electrolyte is continuously circulated to prevent accumulation of impurities. Fixed Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cells have a solid electrolyte that does not move within the cell. Dissolved Fuel Alkaline Fuel Cells use a liquid fuel that is mixed with the electrolyte to produce electricity.</p></p>
