<p><p>Probiotics in animal feed can be categorized into two main types based on their source: bacteria source and yeast source. Bacteria source probiotics typically include lactobacillus and bacillus species, which can promote gut health and improve digestion in animals. On the other hand, yeast source probiotics often consist of strains such as saccharomyces cerevisiae, known for their ability to boost immune function and enhance nutrient absorption. Both types of probiotics play a crucial role in maintaining the overall health and well-being of animals.</p></p>
