<p><p>High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) Transmission Systems refer to systems that transmit electricity over long distances with lower energy losses compared to traditional alternating current systems. There are three main types of HVDC transmission systems - Submarine HVDC Transmission System, which is used to transmit electricity under water bodies, HVDC Overhead Transmission System, which is used for long-distance land transmission, and HVDC Underground Transmission System, which is used to transmit electricity underground in urban areas or where overhead lines are not feasible.</p></p>
