<p><p>The mobile phone display market is divided into two main types: LCD and OLED. LCD displays use a backlight to illuminate the screen, resulting in bright and clear visuals. On the other hand, OLED displays do not require a backlight, allowing for deeper blacks and improved energy efficiency. Both types have their own strengths and weaknesses, with LCD screens being more affordable and widely used, while OLED displays offer better color reproduction and contrast ratios, making them popular in premium smartphones.</p></p>
