<p><p>Smart HVAC controls market types are categorized based on communication range. Short-range systems use technologies like Bluetooth or Zigbee for communication within a limited range, typically within the same room or building. Long-distance systems utilize technologies such as Wi-Fi or cellular networks for communication over larger distances, allowing remote control and monitoring capabilities. Short-range systems are ideal for residential or small commercial applications, while long-distance systems are more suitable for larger commercial or industrial buildings that require remote access and control.</p></p>
