<p><strong>The Tire Pressure Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) can be categorized into two types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors on each tire to directly measure the air pressure and temperature, providing real-time data to the driver. On the other hand, Indirect TPMS calculates tire pressure based on wheel speed and other vehicle parameters. Both systems help to alert drivers of underinflated tires, improving safety and efficiency on the road.</p></p>
