<p><p>Short-wave infrared (SWIR) cameras are categorized into two main types: area cameras and linear cameras. Area cameras capture images over a two-dimensional area, providing detailed information about the whole scene. Linear cameras, on the other hand, capture images in a single line or narrow strip, making them ideal for applications where detailed scanning of objects or surfaces is required. Both types of SWIR cameras are widely used in various industries such as defense, surveillance, medical imaging, and industrial inspection.</p></p>
