<p><p>Mould release oils are used in various industries for the easy release of objects from the mould surface. There are two main types of mould release oils: water-based and oil-based. Water-based mould release oils are environmentally friendly, non-toxic, and easy to clean up. They are suitable for use on most surfaces and provide a smooth release. On the other hand, oil-based mould release oils are more durable and provide a better release for intricate moulds. They are preferred for high-temperature applications and more demanding moulding processes.</p></p>
